How to read the output

`Score` is a weighted hybrid score. `ContentScore` comes from TF-IDF keyword similarity, `SemanticScore` comes from meaning-level embedding similarity, `ProfileScore` comes from your liked seed items, and `PopularityScore` acts as a stable product-quality prior.

`BestSignal` tells you which AI block influenced the recommendation the most. `SignalCount` tells you how many recommendation engines agreed on the same item.
