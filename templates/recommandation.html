How to read the output

`Score` is a weighted hybrid score. `ContentScore` comes from TF-IDF keyword similarity, `SemanticScore` comes from meaning-level embedding similarity, `CollaborativeScore` comes from user-user collaborative filtering, and `PopularityScore` acts as a stable product-quality prior.

`BestSignal` tells you which AI block influenced the recommendation the most. `SignalCount` tells you how many recommendation engines agreed on the same item.

If `Collaborative` appears as `0.0`, it means that item was selected by the content, semantic, or popularity blocks, but it did not receive direct support from the collaborative block for the chosen user.
